# LLVM IR Syntax

- Offical documentation: [LLVM Language Reference Manual](https://llvm.org/docs/LangRef.html)

- label: 基本块标签
  ```llvm
  ; <label>:15:                                     ; preds = %2
  ```
  从前驱基本块%2到当前基本块15的跳转
